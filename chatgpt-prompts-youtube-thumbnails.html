ChatGPT Prompts for Thumbnail Ideas

Stop staring at a blank canvas. Use these prompts to get detailed design concepts, color palettes, and emotional compositions that stop viewers from scrolling.

Prompt 1: The "Visual Hook" Concept Generator

What it does: Breaks down exactly what should be in the foreground, background, and what text should be used to create an instant emotional response.

Act as a professional YouTube thumbnail designer. I am making a video about [Insert Topic]. Give me 5 distinct thumbnail concepts. For each, describe: 1) The main subject/action, 2) The background setting, 3) The specific text (max 3 words) to overlay, and 4) The color scheme to use to make it pop.

Prompt 2: The Before vs. After Transformation

What it does: Creates a visual layout for transformation videos that highlights growth, change, or results.

I am creating a transformation video about [Insert Topic]. Create a thumbnail concept that shows the 'Before' and 'After' clearly. Describe the lighting, the facial expressions for both sides, and how to visually separate the two states so the viewer instantly understands the progress.
