ChatGPT Prompts for Email Copywriting

Write emails that get opened, read, and clicked. These expert copy frameworks help you craft compelling subject lines, high-converting launch sequences, and engaging weekly newsletters.

Prompt 1: The 3-Part High-Converting Sales Sequence

What it does: Generates a structured 3-email sales funnel (The Hook, The Value/Logic, and The Scarcity/Urgency) to pitch a product or service naturally.

Act as an expert direct-response email copywriter. I am launching a [Insert Product/Service] designed to help [Insert Target Audience] achieve [Insert Main Benefit]. Write a 3-part email sequence. Email 1 should focus on a relatable problem and introduce the solution. Email 2 should handle top objections using logic and social proof. Email 3 should introduce a gentle element of urgency or scarcity to drive action. Provide 3 distinct, high-open-rate subject lines for each email.

Prompt 2: The High-Value Weekly Newsletter Template

What it does: Builds a highly engaging storytelling newsletter that teaches a lesson while subtly pointing readers back to your business or offers.

Act as a professional newsletter publisher. I need a weekly value-first email newsletter for an audience interested in [Insert Industry/Topic]. Write an engaging email that uses a brief, interesting story or analogy to teach a valuable lesson, breaks down 3 quick bulleted action steps, and ends with a smooth transition to a soft pitch for my business services.
