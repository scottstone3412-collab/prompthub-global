ChatGPT Prompts for YouTube SEO & Tags

Rank on page one of search results. Use these optimized prompts to generate algorithm-friendly descriptions, strategic keyword tags, and optimized video timelines.

Prompt 1: The Smart Description & Timestamps Builder

What it does: Writes a keyword-rich video description that helps YouTube understand your content while organizing your video into clean, clickable chapters.

Act as a YouTube SEO expert. I am uploading a video about [Insert Topic]. Write a 3-paragraph video description optimized for search algorithms. The first paragraph must contain primary keywords naturally. Also, include placeholder sections for 5 chronological timestamps/chapters, a call-to-action to subscribe, and links to social media.

Prompt 2: The Viral Tag & Keyword Generator

What it does: Extracts high-volume, low-competition search terms to paste into your YouTube video tags box.

Generate a list of 20 highly relevant, search-optimized tags for a YouTube video titled "[Insert Video Title]". Categorize them into broad keywords, specific long-tail keywords, and common misspellings or alternative phrases that users might type into the search bar.
