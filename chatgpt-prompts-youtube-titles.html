ChatGPT Prompts for YouTube Titles

Fix your Click-Through Rate (CTR) with psychological, attention-grabbing hooks. These tested prompts analyze viral patterns to turn boring video topics into unstoppable click magnets.

Prompt 1: The Curiosity Gap Title Generator

What it does: Uses psychology to construct open loops that make it nearly impossible for a viewer to scroll past without clicking to find the answer.

Best use case: Documentaries, storytelling channels, true crime, or deep-dive educational videos.

Act as a viral YouTube consultant. I will give you my video topic, and you will generate 10 curiosity-gap style titles under 50 characters. These titles must state a shocking or intriguing fact but leave out the core answer so the viewer must watch to find out. Do not use generic clickbait phrases; keep them grounded, mysterious, and highly clickable. 

My Video Topic: [Insert your video topic here]

Example Output Snapshot

"Why This Ancient Village Disappeared Without a Trace..."

Prompt 2: The "X vs Y" Extreme Comparison Formula

What it does: Pitches two contrasting elements against each other to drive instant human intrigue and healthy debate in the comments.

Best use case: Product reviews, tech battles, financial strategies, or fitness challenges.

Act as an expert YouTube growth specialist. Generate 8 highly competitive 'X vs Y' comparison style titles based on my topic. Use high-contrast power words (e.g., Useless, Secret, Master, Beginner). Ensure the titles create a dramatic showdown feel that forces the viewer to pick a side.

My Video Topic: [Insert your video topic here]

Example Output Snapshot

"Free AI Tools vs $500 Software: The Truth Shocked Me"
