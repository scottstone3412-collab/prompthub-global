ChatGPT Prompts for Social Media Marketing

Stop staring at a blank content calendar. Use these highly tactical prompts to generate viral caption hooks, structure multi-day content plans, and turn random followers into paying clients.

Prompt 1: The 7-Day High-Engagement Content Calendar

What it does: Builds a balanced weekly content plan mixing educational value, promotional offers, and highly relatable behind-the-scenes stories.

Act as an expert social media manager and growth strategist. I run a business providing [Insert Product/Service] for [Insert Target Audience]. Generate a highly engaging 7-day content calendar for Instagram and Facebook. For each day, provide: 1) The Content Pillar (e.g., Value, Authority, Promotion), 2) A visual layout suggestion (e.g., Reel, Carousel, Photo), and 3) A compelling, short caption starter with a clear Call to Action (CTA).

Prompt 2: The Scroll-Stopping Caption & Hook Creator

What it does: Crafts attention-grabbing short captions specifically engineered for short-form videos like TikTok or Reels to increase your video retention rates.

Act as a professional copywriter. I am making a short-form video/post about [Insert Specific Topic/Tip]. Generate 5 scroll-stopping opening hooks under 10 words, followed by an engaging, conversational caption layout that uses bullet points for easy reading. Include 5 highly relevant, non-spammy hashtags.
